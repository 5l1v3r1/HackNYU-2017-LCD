Superb Features

Cost-cutting

LCD enables hospitals to reduce headcount for CT specialists in residence while serving more patients. The fixed purchasing cost of LCD is more cost-effective as compared with exponentially growing salaries for doctors over time.

Time-saving

LCD only requires 2 weeks of deep learning whilst human doctors need 11-17 years of professional training. Meanwhile with the assistance of LCD, CT specialists can focus on highlighted cancer modules and reduce consultation time for each patient without compromising accuracy.

User-friendly

LCD has an elegant and concise user interface, with only one action needed from the users - upload CT scans. The clear instruction and high process efficiency illustrate the beauty of machine learning in real life, showcasing technology’s contributions on healthcare.
